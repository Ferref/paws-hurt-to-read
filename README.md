## Paws Hurt To Read

### Requirements plan (MVP)
Users can

- Register (with email and password oauth etc later)
- Login
- Logout

- Download books from the provided catalog
- Upload their own books (books are uploaded into a repository)
- Read books

- Get a report about
    - Downloaded books
    - Most read books
    - Favourite categories
    - Most common words in their books

    - Other books they would love (Not just keyword matching but deep analysis)
